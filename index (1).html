About this campaign

Across the globe, thousands of species face extinction due to habitat destruction, poaching, and climate change. This campaign shines a light on the urgent need to protect our planet's most vulnerable wildlife — from the majestic elephant to the elusive snow leopard. Together, we can act before it is too late.

Want to make a difference? Visit the World Wildlife Fund to learn how you can help protect endangered species today.
